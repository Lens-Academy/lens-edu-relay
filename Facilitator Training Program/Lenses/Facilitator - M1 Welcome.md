#### Text
content:: Module 1 — *Foundations*. **Time: ~30 minutes. Format: 3 short readings + 1 reflection exercise.**

Before you learn how to facilitate, you need to know what a facilitator actually **is** — and what they aren't. This module establishes the role: how it differs from teaching, the mindset it operates from, where its responsibilities end, and two postures experienced facilitators reference.

By the end of this module you should be able to: state the core distinction between teaching and facilitating in your own words, name the trigger most likely to pull *you* out of facilitator mode, and articulate two specific things you are *not* responsible for as a facilitator.

When you're ready, head to lesson 1.1.
