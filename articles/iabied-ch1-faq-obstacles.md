## But aren't there big obstacles to reaching superintelligence?

The field lacks complete understanding of what barriers remain. Progress could continue through minor adjustments to current techniques, or require breakthrough architectures like those that transformed AI in the past (AlexNet, AlphaGo, transformers).

### Historical Pattern of Overcoming Challenges

The AI field has consistently surmounted major obstacles:

- **Image recognition**: AIs struggled for decades until 2012, when AlexNet enabled modern deep learning
- **Complex games**: Go seemed insurmountable until AlphaGo combined neural networks with tree search in 2016
- **Natural language**: Early chatbots had severe limitations until transformer architecture maturation produced GPT-3 and ChatGPT

### Current Bottlenecks

As of mid-2025, AIs show weaknesses in long-term memory and consistent planning, demonstrated by difficulty playing Pokemon. Yet researchers actively identify and address such limitations rather than accepting them as permanent constraints.

### Scaling and Research Growth

Private AI investment has increased over twentyfold since 2012. Major conferences have grown 9-10 times larger. This expanded research community makes obstacle-overcoming more likely than previously.

### The Unknowable Timeline

Without understanding intelligence's fundamental nature, predicting when major breakthroughs occur remains impossible. The next architectural insight could arrive soon or require years of development.

**Conclusion**: Obstacles likely exist, but the field's track record suggests researchers will probably overcome them unless humanity intervenes through policy.
