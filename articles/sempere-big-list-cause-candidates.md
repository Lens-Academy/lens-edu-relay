## Introduction

This post lists cause candidates that have been suggested on the EA Forum, organized by category. It is meant as a resource for cause prioritization research. The author notes: "I've pretty much just gone through the EA Forum and pulled together cause candidates. I haven't evaluated them."

The list draws on EA Forum posts, the EA Wiki, and related sources. Categories include animal welfare, community building, global health, existential risk, rationality, and many more.

---

## Animal Welfare and Suffering

1. **Reducing Wild Animal Suffering from Natural Causes (e.g., Wildfires)** — reducing suffering caused by natural disasters affecting wild animals
2. **Invertebrate Welfare** — taking seriously the possibility that invertebrates are sentient
3. **Humane Insecticides** — developing pesticides that kill insects more humanely
4. **Diet Change (away from factory farming)** — shifting human diets to reduce farmed animal suffering
5. **Vegan Recidivism** — reducing the rate at which people who go vegan revert to eating meat
6. **Plant-Based Seafood** — developing plant-based alternatives to seafood
7. **Plant-Based Diets as Climate Strategy** — advocating plant-based diets on climate grounds as an entry point to animal welfare
8. **Moral Circle Expansion** — expanding the set of beings whose interests are considered morally relevant
9. **Analgesics for Farm Animals** — providing pain relief to animals undergoing painful procedures on farms
10. **Welfare of Specific Animals** — focusing on particular species with especially poor welfare (e.g., broiler chickens, laying hens)
11. **Cell-Based Meat R&D** — accelerating the development of cultivated meat
12. **Animal-Free Proteins** — developing non-animal protein sources more broadly
13. **Antibiotic Resistance (from livestock)** — reducing antibiotic use in animal agriculture
14. **Vaccination of Wild Animals** — vaccinating wild animal populations against disease
15. **Herbivorizing Predators** — the speculative idea of reducing predation in nature

## Methodology (Appendix I)

The author compiled this list by searching the EA Forum for posts tagged as cause candidates, as well as by browsing through cause-related tags and categories. The list is intended to be comprehensive rather than evaluative — causes are included based on having been proposed in an EA Forum post, not based on the author's view of their merit.

## Nomenclature (Appendix II)

The author distinguishes between:
- **Cause area**: A broad domain of concern (e.g., global health)
- **Cause**: A more specific problem within a cause area (e.g., malaria prevention)
- **Intervention**: A specific action to address a cause (e.g., distributing bed nets)

The list primarily catalogues cause-level candidates, though some entries are closer to cause areas and others closer to interventions.
