The navigator training course is ready for review! We're sharing it with a small group of experienced navigators to get your input before the course launches on June 22.

**Setup:** Open two windows side by side. On the left, open the course in the editor: [editor link](https://editor.lensacademy.org/edu/6903c363). On the right, open the course on the platform: [platform link](https://staging.lensacademy.org/course/facilitator-training-program-flat). Read and interact with the course on the platform (right) so you experience it the way a learner would, including the AI tutor conversations. Leave your feedback in the editor (left).

**To leave a comment in the editor:** Right-click on the relevant text and select comment (or Cmd+Shift+M). For small corrections like typos or wording changes, just type directly in the editor and it will create a suggestion for us to approve. If someone else has already commented on a section, reply directly to their comment rather than creating a new one.

**What to comment on:**

- **Corrections**: something in the course is wrong or misleading. Say what and why.
- **Experience conflicts**: the course recommends something that contradicts what actually works in practice. Share what you've seen work instead and in what context.
- **Gaps**: the course is missing something important that a new facilitator needs to know.
- **Fluff**: content that isn't helping and could be cut.

**For the AI tutor sections:** interact with the tutor on the platform side first, then leave your feedback on the AI Tutor Instructions block in the editor. If the tutor did something unhelpful, missed the point of the reflection, or could be prompted better, say so there.

**Deadline: June 22nd.** Please do a full read-through this week as part of your prep for navigating the course. How deep you go is up to your bandwidth, the more thorough the better!
