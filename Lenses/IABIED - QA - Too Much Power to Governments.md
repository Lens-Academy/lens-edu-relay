#### Text
content::
The authors argue that banning one additional technology won't plunge the world into totalitarianism, any more than nuclear arms treaties did. Governments already regulate vast numbers of technologies. The ban they propose would also apply to governments themselves -- they aren't calling for state-built superintelligence, but for the technology not to be built at all.
