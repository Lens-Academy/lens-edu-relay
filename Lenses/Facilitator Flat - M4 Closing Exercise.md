#### Text
content:: **Practical exercise (5 min)**

Pick one of your next session's planned questions. For that question, write out:

1. **The PPPB version:** how would you pose it, what's the pause length, who would you pounce on first?
2. **One possible answer a participant might give.** Now decide: which of the 7 taking-shares moves would you use in response? Why?
3. **One alternative move** that could also work, and what's different about choosing it.

This is the difference between knowing the moves and being able to use them. You don't have to share this — even doing it for one question makes a difference in your first session.
