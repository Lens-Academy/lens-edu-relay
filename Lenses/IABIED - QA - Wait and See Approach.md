#### Text
content::
The authors argue that a wait-and-see approach is impossible because nobody knows where the critical capability thresholds are. AI could get out of control once it's smart enough to automate AI research, and that could happen quietly in a lab. Humanity is sprinting toward a cliff edge in the dark and fog -- we can't wait until we fall off to decide we should have stopped.
