#### Text
content::
This extended discussion sketches the practical mechanics of a global AI shutdown across three dimensions: preventing new chip production (feasible due to extreme supply chain concentration), preventing chip usage (large datacenters are visible from satellites), and preventing algorithmic progress (making research illegal would deter 99.9% of people). The authors stress the window is closing -- the longer we wait, the harder enforcement becomes.
