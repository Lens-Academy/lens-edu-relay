#### Text
content::
The authors argue that stopping AI development doesn't require surveillance of ordinary computers. Advanced AI training requires enormous specialized datacenters and chips made by only a few manufacturers. Key chip fabrication technology comes from a single company (ASML in the Netherlands). You can turn off the supply at the source -- but only if you act soon, before the supply chain diversifies.
