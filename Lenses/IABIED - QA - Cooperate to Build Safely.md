#### Text
content::
The authors argue that an international collaboration of alchemists can't transmute lead into gold any more than a single alchemist can. They worry that institutional dynamics -- the pressure to approve research, the embarrassment of repeated delays -- would inevitably push a cooperative AI institute past safety limits, as happened with Chernobyl and the Challenger.
