#### Text
content:: Most of what makes a session work is decided before anyone joins the call.

**Time:** 25 minutes
**Format:** 1 short reading + 2 frameworks + 1 reflection

This module is about how to prepare for a session you're about to run, how to recognise the shapes of a discussion, and a few facilitator-level moves the template can't fully prescribe (norms, cameras, chat).
