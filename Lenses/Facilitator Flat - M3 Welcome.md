#### Text
content:: Module 3 — *Designing & Opening a Session*. **Time: ~35 minutes. Format: 3 short readings + 2 frameworks + 1 practical exercise.**

Most of what makes a session work is decided before anyone joins the call. This module covers the prep that actually moves quality, the three-phase shape almost every good session has, how to set cohort norms in Week 1 (with the cohort, not at them), and the first 10 minutes of a session. The next module, M4, covers what happens after.

By the end you should be able to: identify which prep moves actually matter (and which are busywork); describe the Diamond-of-Participation shape; and draft an opening plan for your first session.
