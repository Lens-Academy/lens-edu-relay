#### Text
content:: **Closing exercise (5 min)**

Write a paragraph (5–8 sentences) answering, in your own words:

What is the job of a facilitator, and how is it different from a teacher? What's one situation in which I'm likely to drift out of the facilitator role, and what's the trigger?

You don't have to share this with anyone. Writing it is the point — if you can't put the distinction into your own words, you don't yet have it. If you can, you've got the foundation for the next four modules.
