#### Text
content::
The authors argue a research ban is necessary because better algorithms are just as dangerous as better hardware -- "two horses drawing the same cart off a cliff." A single paper in 2017 kicked off the entire LLM revolution. If AI ever becomes trainable on consumer hardware, enforcement becomes nearly impossible. Making it illegal would deter 99.9% of people, with the remainder handled by law enforcement.
