#### Text
content::
The authors argue humanity already regulates and slows down many technologies -- from nuclear fission to hair styling. They say it would be "truly tragic and absurd" if society used its regulatory power on medicine, housing, and energy but neglected to use it on one of the rare technologies that would kill everyone. A ban can be narrowly targeted: most people wouldn't feel the effects.
