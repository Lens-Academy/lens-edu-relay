#### Text
content::
The authors provide extended critiques of three alignment plans discussed in the book. Musk's "truth-seeking" AI (Grok) has already publicly misfired, producing antisemitic content despite instructions to be truthful. LeCun's "submissive AI" plan is vague and contradicted by the fact that companies are already giving agency to AIs he said they never would. OpenAI's "superalignment" idea of having AIs solve alignment fails because humans can't verify whether an AI's proposed solution is correct or a trap. The authors also argue that AI labs' apparent incompetence in safety is real, not a cover for secret competence.
