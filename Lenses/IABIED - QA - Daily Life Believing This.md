content::
The authors describe how believing in imminent AI risk dramatically affects their priorities -- Soares took a one-third pay cut in 2014 to work on the problem, and Yudkowsky has dedicated his life to it since 2000. They push back on the idea that "if you really believed this you'd do X crazy thing," arguing that living in the shadow of annihilation doesn't have to make you stupid.
