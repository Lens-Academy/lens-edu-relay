#### Text
content::
The authors argue that pitting AIs against each other -- having them debate, compete, or monitor one another -- won't save us. If you can't get one AI to reliably work for humanity, adding more AIs just adds more failure points and more opportunities for collusion. They compare it to children trying to govern a city of sociopaths: the sociopaths will eventually figure out how to cooperate against the children, especially when the stakes are existential.
