#### Text
content::
The authors argue that AI risk is not a distraction from other global threats like nuclear war or bioterrorism -- the world is big enough for multiple serious problems. What makes AI uniquely pressing is that tens of billions of dollars are actively being poured into making it more capable, unlike other existential threats where nobody is racing to build superviruses.
