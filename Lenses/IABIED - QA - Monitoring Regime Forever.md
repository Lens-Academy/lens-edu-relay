#### Text
content::
The authors acknowledge that a monitoring regime can't last forever -- eventually researchers will find more efficient methods or rogue actors will subvert the ban. But they argue humanity doesn't need to buy time forever: the goal is to buy decades for biotechnology to augment human intelligence enough that future researchers could actually solve the alignment problem. The monitoring regime shouldn't last forever either -- humanity's descendants deserve to reach the stars.
