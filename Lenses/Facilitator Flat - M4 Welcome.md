content:: Module 4 — *Running the Session: In-the-Moment Craft*. **Time: ~40 minutes. Format: 4 frameworks + 2 short videos + practice prompts.**

You're in the room. People are looking at you. What do you actually do?

This is the longest module because it covers the steady-state craft — the moves you make every 3-5 minutes during a session. You'll re-read parts of it before your first few sessions. Six topics: holding four perspectives at once, reading the room, asking questions that produce thinking, taking shares, engaging quiet participants, and keeping engagement over a full session.

By the end you should be able to: name which of the four facilitator perspectives you tend to neglect; describe the Pose-Pause-Pounce-Bounce question pattern; and pick the right "taking shares" move from a list of seven for a given situation.
