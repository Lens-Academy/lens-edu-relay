#### Text
content:: Facilitation works only if discussion produces learning. Most people assume it does. The evidence is more specific than that.

**Time:** 35 minutes
**Format:** 2 readings + 1 short framework + 1 reflection exercise

This module gives you the empirical case behind the format. You'll need it for two reasons: (1) so you can explain to participants — when they ask — *why* we don't just summarise the reading at the top; and (2) so when a session feels slow or chaotic, you can tell whether it's working despite the feeling, or actually failing.
