#### Text
content::
The authors argue that lack of expert consensus is itself a sign of an immature technical field, not a sign that things are fine. Of the three Turing Award winners who kicked off modern AI, two (Hinton, Bengio) warn of existential danger and one (LeCun) dismisses it. This level of disagreement about how a machine operates is abnormal for a mature technical field -- imagine physicists arguing about basic principles while creating larger and larger explosions.
