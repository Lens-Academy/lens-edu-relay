#### Text
content::
The authors reject the strategy of hiding extinction concerns behind safer-sounding issues like job loss or deepfakes. They give four reasons: it's dishonest and people can tell, it's probably unnecessary since elected officials are already receptive to direct discussion, it doesn't actually address the superintelligence problem, and time may be too short for a gradual approach. They report that frank DC conversations about extinction risk have gone "encouragingly well."
