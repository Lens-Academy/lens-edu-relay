#### Text
content::
The authors compile statements from officials across both U.S. political parties and the UN Secretary-General showing growing recognition of AI existential risk. Quotes range from Trump ("we have to watch it") to Blumenthal (warning of AGI within 1-3 years that could "deceive us, manipulate us") to Guterres calling AI "an existential threat to humanity on a par with the risk of nuclear war." The authors argue the major impediment is education, not persuadability.
