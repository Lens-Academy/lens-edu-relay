#### Text
content::
The authors argue that if national leaders truly understood that superintelligence is suicide rather than a path to military dominance, they would desperately want to sign a treaty. Even North Korea, which developed nuclear weapons illegally, hasn't *used* them. A coalition of world powers that genuinely believes their survival is at stake would create overwhelming pressure on any holdout nation. Nuclear non-proliferation has worked for 80+ years.
