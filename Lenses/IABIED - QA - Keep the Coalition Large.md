#### Text
content::
The authors argue against bundling other AI issues (art, deepfakes, drone warfare) with the superintelligence ban. The coalition needs to be as large as possible, including people with diverse views on these other topics. They draw a parallel to Cold War nuclear cooperation: the East and West treated ideological disagreement and nuclear annihilation as different problems, cooperating on the latter while competing on the former. Nothing should be packaged with the survival of humanity.
