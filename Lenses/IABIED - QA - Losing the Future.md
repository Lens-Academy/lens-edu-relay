#### Text
content::
The authors worry that a superintelligent AI takeover wouldn't just kill humanity — it would extinguish joy, consciousness, and meaning from the reachable universe entirely. Since consciousness is probably not the most efficient way to do cognitive work, a superintelligence would have no reason to preserve or create it. The future could be a vast expanse of Dyson shells harvesting starlight with nobody and nothing happy about it.
