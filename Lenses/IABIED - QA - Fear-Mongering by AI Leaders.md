#### Text
content::
The authors argue that whether CEOs are fear-mongering for profit is irrelevant to whether AI is actually dangerous -- you have to evaluate the technical arguments, not the corporate incentives. They note that researchers with zero corporate incentives were warning about these dangers years before the current companies existed, and that many independent academics and public figures now share these concerns.
