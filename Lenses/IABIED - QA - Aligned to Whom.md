#### Text
content::
The authors argue this thorny philosophical question is currently moot -- humanity can't get *any* goals into an AI reliably, so debating *which* goals is a distraction. We don't need to agree on "aligned to whom" to coordinate on a ban, because most people agree the destruction of all life is bad. They warn against letting philosophical debates about ideal alignment derail urgent action on survival.
