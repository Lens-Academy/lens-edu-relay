#### Text
content:: **Final exercise (5 min)**

Two sentences in your own words:

1. **What's my default failure mode under pressure (pufferfish or hermit crab), and what's one move I'll try the first time I notice it happening?**
2. **What's the close I want to use for my sessions — the small consistent ritual?**

That's the end of the course.
