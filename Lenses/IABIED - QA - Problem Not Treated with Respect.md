#### Text
content::
The authors contrast AI companies' approach to safety with how serious engineering disciplines operate. Air traffic control produces 200-page postmortem reports analyzing every fatal accident down to individual fan blade fracture mechanics. NASA requires at most a 1-in-270 chance of killing a crew of volunteers. Manhattan Project scientists set a threshold of 3-in-a-million for existential risk. Meanwhile, AI lab CEOs publicly estimate 10-25% chances of civilizational catastrophe and keep building anyway, offering platitudes like "GPT-4 is our most aligned model yet."
