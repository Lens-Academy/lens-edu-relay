#### Text
content::
Could we solve alignment by making a deal with an AI — letting it grow powerful in exchange for protecting humanity? The authors argue no: AIs won't have human-style "honor" (which is an evolutionarily contingent hack), and once superintelligent, they'd have no practical reason to keep promises since humans can't punish or even verify their commitments. Even game-theoretic cooperation requires each party to read the other's mind — something humans cannot do with AIs.
