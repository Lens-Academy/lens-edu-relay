#### Text
content::
The authors argue the alignment problem isn't fundamentally unsolvable -- humans just aren't smart enough to solve it reliably. Humans augmented beyond Einstein's level might notice their own cognitive biases, stop being systematically overconfident, and actually solve alignment on the first try. Unlike AI, enhanced humans come "pre-aligned" with prosocial motivations. But this is a plan for after shutting down AI companies -- not a replacement for that first step.
