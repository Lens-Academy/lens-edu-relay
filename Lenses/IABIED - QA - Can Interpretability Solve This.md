#### Text
content::
This Q&A addresses whether reading an AI's thoughts — through interpretability research or reasoning traces — could solve the alignment problem. The authors argue that we're currently bad at reading AI thoughts, that the problem gets harder as AIs get smarter, and that even if we could catch dangerous thoughts, we wouldn't know how to fix the underlying issue without just training the AI to hide better.
