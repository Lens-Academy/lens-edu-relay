#### Text
content::
The authors acknowledge the cost of restricting chip ownership but argue the tradeoff is clear: erring too low on chip limits hampers a few wealthy hobbyists (nine H100 GPUs cost at least $270,000), while erring too high risks everyone's death. They also warn this chip-based enforcement window won't last forever -- more efficient algorithms could eventually make the restriction moot.
