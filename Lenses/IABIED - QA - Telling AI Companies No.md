content::
The authors caution policymakers against plans that require AI company CEOs to say "yes." If Sam Altman tried to shut down OpenAI tomorrow, Microsoft would replace him. The right plans are ones the heads of AI companies will vociferously object to. If AI companies retain the authority to choose to destroy the world, then the world ends on full automatic.
