#### Text
content::
Chapter 4 argues that training doesn't produce intended goals. This Q&A tackles the hopeful idea that smarter AIs will self-correct toward benevolence. The response argues that an AI will fix what *it* sees as flaws — and an AI with alien drives would "fix" itself in directions that move further from human values, not closer. Self-modification amplifies existing misalignment rather than correcting it.
