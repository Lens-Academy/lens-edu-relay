#### Text
content::
The authors argue that clear warning shots are unlikely, and that humanity is not prepared to learn from them even if they come. They compare AI labs unfavorably to NASA and the FAA, which could trace failures to specific causes. When AI behaves unexpectedly, labs just retrain until the bad behavior is less visible. They also argue humanity's track record with "warning shots" (e.g., COVID and gain-of-function research) is poor.
