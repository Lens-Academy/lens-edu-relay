assessment-instructions::
**1** — Can't name three mechanisms. Treats the question as theoretical.

**2** — Names three mechanisms but cannot connect them to personal experience or to facilitator moves.

**3** — Names three with brief personal examples (good or bad learning) and at least one concrete facilitator move per mechanism (e.g., "generation effect → ask 'what do you think this means?' before offering an interpretation").

**4** — As above, plus articulates *why* discussion is harder than re-reading (desirable difficulty) — that the discomfort is a feature, not a bug, and explaining this to a participant who complains about session difficulty is part of the facilitator's job.

**5** — As above, plus connects "worked examples → faded scaffolding" to the arc of a multi-week course: more facilitator structure in Week 1, less by Week 6.
