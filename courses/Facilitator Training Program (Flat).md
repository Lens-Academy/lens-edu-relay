title: Facilitator Training Program (Flat)
description: "A hands-on program for anyone who facilitates discussion-based learning — cohort facilitators, reading-group leads, and educators who want the group to do the thinking. Across five modules it builds the craft end to end: the mindset and postures that separate facilitating from teaching; how people actually learn and why discussion works; how to prepare and open a session; the in-the-moment skills of running one — reading the room, asking questions that produce thinking, taking shares, and drawing out quiet participants; and how to handle hard moments, coach one-on-one, and close with intention. Each module runs as a single continuous flow — Welcome → lessons → optional resources — with every optional video, article, and reference as its own lens so progress on each is tracked."
